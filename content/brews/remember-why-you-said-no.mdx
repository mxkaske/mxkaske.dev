---
title: Remember Why You Said No
date: 2025-05-16
description: It's easy to forget why you said no.
---

It’s easy to remember why you said yes - you live through it, invest time, and chase a certain result.

But when you say no, life moves on. There's no outcome, no memory loop.

That’s why it’s important to remember why you said no — so you don’t forget the reason just because it didn’t unfold.
